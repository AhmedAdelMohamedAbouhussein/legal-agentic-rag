FIXED-PRICE CONSTRUCTION CONTRACT
Contract No.: CC-2022-0087

This Construction Agreement is entered into as of March 1, 2022, between:
PINECREST DEVELOPMENTS LLC ("Owner") and IRONBRIDGE GENERAL CONTRACTING INC. ("Contractor").

1. PROJECT

Construction of a 4-story commercial office building at 550 Harbor Blvd, Miami, FL 33132. Architect: Meridian Design Group. Architect's drawings dated February 2022 are incorporated by reference.

2. CONTRACT PRICE

Fixed price: USD $5,000,000. This is a stipulated sum contract. Contractor bears all cost risk above the contract price except for Owner-directed changes.

3. CONTRACT DOCUMENTS

In order of precedence: (1) This Agreement; (2) AIA General Conditions A201-2017; (3) Supplementary Conditions; (4) Specifications; (5) Drawings; (6) Addenda.

4. CHANGE ORDERS

4.1 DEFINITION: A Change Order is a written instrument signed by both Owner and Contractor stating the agreed-upon change in scope, contract price adjustment, and schedule adjustment.
4.2 REQUIREMENT: No change in scope, price, or schedule is effective unless documented in a signed Change Order. Verbal instructions from Owner or Owner's representatives do NOT constitute binding Change Orders.
4.3 CHANGE ORDER REQUEST (COR): If Owner requests a change, Contractor shall submit a COR within 10 business days with cost and schedule impact. Owner has 10 business days to approve, reject, or negotiate.
4.4 CONSTRUCTIVE CHANGE: If Contractor believes an Owner action constitutes a constructive change order, Contractor must provide written notice within 7 days or waive the claim.
4.5 CARDINAL CHANGES: Changes that fundamentally alter the scope of work or that cumulatively exceed 20% of the original contract price may entitle Contractor to treat the project as a cardinal change.

5. SCOPE OF WORK

Contractor shall furnish all labor, materials, equipment, and supervision necessary to complete the Work as defined in the Contract Documents. Any work beyond the Contract Documents requires a signed Change Order.

6. SCHEDULE

Notice to Proceed: March 15, 2022. Substantial Completion: December 15, 2022. Final Completion: January 31, 2023.

7. LIQUIDATED DAMAGES

If Contractor fails to achieve Substantial Completion by December 15, 2022, Contractor shall pay Owner liquidated damages of $2,500 per calendar day of delay. The parties agree this represents a genuine pre-estimate of the Owner's daily loss from delayed occupancy.

8. PAYMENTS

Monthly progress payments based on Schedule of Values. Owner may withhold 10% retainage until Substantial Completion.

9. UNJUST ENRICHMENT

Where Contractor performs work at Owner's specific direction that is later disputed as outside scope, Owner shall not be permitted to retain the benefit of such work without compensation. The doctrine of unjust enrichment may apply where Owner directed and accepted the work, even absent a formal Change Order.

10. DISPUTES

Claims must be submitted within 21 days of the event giving rise to the claim. Disputes not resolved by the Architect's initial decision proceed to mediation, then binding arbitration under AAA Construction Industry Rules.

11. TERMINATION

Owner may terminate for convenience upon 7 days' notice. Contractor shall be compensated for work completed, costs incurred, overhead, and a 5% profit on work performed.

12. GOVERNING LAW: State of Florida.
