MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Mutual NDA is entered into as of September 1, 2022, between MERIDIAN ANALYTICS INC. ("Party A") and GLOBALTECH SOLUTIONS LTD. ("Party B").

1. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one party to the other, including: trade secrets, customer lists, pricing structures, deal pipeline information, financial projections, technical specifications, and product roadmaps. Customer lists, pricing structures, and deal pipeline information are specifically included.

2. EXCLUSIONS

Does not include: (a) publicly known information; (b) independently developed information; (c) information received lawfully from a third party; (d) information required by law to be disclosed.

3. OBLIGATIONS

The Receiving Party shall: (a) hold Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) use it solely for evaluating a potential business relationship; (c) not disclose to third parties without written consent; (d) not use for competitive purposes or to solicit the Disclosing Party's customers; (e) promptly notify of any unauthorised disclosure.

4. TERM

Agreement effective for 3 years. Obligations regarding trade secrets survive indefinitely. All other obligations survive for 5 years from date of disclosure.

5. RETURN OR DESTRUCTION

Upon request, Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information within 10 business days.

6. INJUNCTIVE RELIEF

Breach would cause irreparable harm. Either party may seek injunctive relief without bond in addition to other remedies.

7. DTSA NOTICE

Pursuant to 18 U.S.C. 1833(b): An individual shall not be held criminally or civilly liable for disclosure of a trade secret made in confidence to a government official or attorney for reporting a suspected violation of law.

8. GOVERNING LAW: State of New York.
