SAAS ENTERPRISE SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT
Agreement ID: SAAS-ENT-2021-00892

This SaaS Enterprise Subscription Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2021, between:

CLOUDOPS PLATFORM INC., a Delaware corporation ("Vendor")
and
ACME STARTUP LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Customer")

1. SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES

1.1 Vendor grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable subscription to access and use the CloudOps Platform ("Services") as described in Order Form No. 892 attached hereto.
1.2 Subscription Tier: Enterprise Plus
1.3 Permitted Users: Up to 150 named users
1.4 Initial Subscription Term: January 15, 2021 – January 14, 2022

2. FEES AND PAYMENT

2.1 Annual Subscription Fee: USD $120,000 per year, billed annually in advance.
2.2 Payment is due within 30 days of invoice.
2.3 Overdue payments accrue interest at 1.5% per month.
2.4 All fees are non-refundable except as expressly stated herein.

3. IMPLEMENTATION AND ONBOARDING

3.1 Vendor shall provide up to 40 hours of onboarding support at no additional charge.
3.2 Customer is responsible for user training and administration.

4. SERVICE LEVELS

4.1 Vendor commits to 99.5% monthly uptime excluding scheduled maintenance.
4.2 Service credits for downtime: 10% of monthly fee per hour below SLA (capped at 30% per month).

5. DATA AND PRIVACY

5.1 Customer owns all Customer data uploaded to the Services.
5.2 Vendor shall process Customer data only as a data processor as described in the DPA (Exhibit C).
5.3 Vendor shall maintain SOC 2 Type II certification and provide reports upon request.

6. ACCEPTABLE USE

6.1 Customer shall not: (a) reverse engineer the Services; (b) use the Services for illegal purposes; (c) share login credentials; (d) sublicense or resell access.

7. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

7.1 Vendor retains all intellectual property rights in the Services, platform, and underlying technology.
7.2 Customer retains all rights in Customer data and Customer-created content.
7.3 Vendor may use anonymised, aggregated Customer data to improve its services.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

8.1 Both parties agree to keep confidential any non-public information disclosed in connection with this Agreement.

9. WARRANTIES

9.1 Vendor warrants that the Services will perform materially in accordance with the Documentation.
9.2 DISCLAIMER: EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH HEREIN, THE SERVICES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS" WITHOUT WARRANTY OF ANY KIND.

10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

10.1 NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES.
10.2 VENDOR'S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE FEES PAID BY CUSTOMER IN THE 12 MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

11. INDEMNIFICATION

11.1 Vendor shall indemnify Customer against third-party claims that the Services infringe any intellectual property right.
11.2 Customer shall indemnify Vendor against claims arising from Customer's data or breach of this Agreement.

12. TERM AND RENEWAL

12.1 Initial Term: This Agreement commences on January 15, 2021 and expires on January 14, 2022 ("Initial Term").
12.2 Automatic Renewal: UNLESS EITHER PARTY PROVIDES WRITTEN NOTICE OF NON-RENEWAL AT LEAST NINETY (90) DAYS PRIOR TO THE END OF THE THEN-CURRENT TERM, THIS AGREEMENT SHALL AUTOMATICALLY RENEW FOR SUCCESSIVE ONE-YEAR TERMS AT THE THEN-CURRENT SUBSCRIPTION FEE, WHICH MAY BE ADJUSTED BY VENDOR BY UP TO 10% WITH 60 DAYS' PRIOR NOTICE.
12.3 Notice of Non-Renewal must be delivered via email to renewals@cloudops.io and via first-class mail to Vendor's registered address.
12.4 Customer acknowledges receipt and review of this automatic renewal provision.

13. TERMINATION FOR CAUSE

13.1 Either party may terminate upon 30 days' written notice of material breach if such breach is not cured within the notice period.
13.2 Upon termination for cause by Customer, Vendor shall refund pro-rated unused fees.

14. EFFECTS OF TERMINATION

14.1 Upon termination, Customer's access to the Services shall be immediately disabled.
14.2 Vendor shall retain Customer data for 30 days post-termination, after which it may be deleted.
14.3 Customer may export data during the 30-day retention period.

15. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

15.1 Disputes shall first be subject to 30 days of senior management negotiation.
15.2 Unresolved disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration under AAA Commercial Rules in San Francisco, CA.

16. GOVERNING LAW

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
